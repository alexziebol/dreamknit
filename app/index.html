What does this application do?

You can submit objects you found in your dream in addition to a short description of what your current mental state & situation is. You're also able to see submissions made by others to see if there are any similarities between yours and others' dreams.

What does this hope to achieve?

I'm hoping that specific objects found in dreams can be tied to specific mental states and environmental situations.

Why was this made?

I started this as a mini-project to practice Javascript, Sass, PHP and mySQL! I will slowly add functionalities as I learn the languages.
